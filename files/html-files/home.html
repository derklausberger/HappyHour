What is Happy hour?

Find Bars in your near Location

Happy Hour shows you bars near you. You will get 20 bars suggested and you can easily choose which one you want to go to. In addition to a picture, you can also see the ratings and opening hours. All at a glance!

Find new Cocktailrecipes

Happy Hour offers you a large database of cheap and delicious cocktails. You can choose and make cocktails from A-Z. You can see both the ingredients and the recipe, and you can like and comment on them.
